Research Experience

LLM Post-training & Latent Reasoning · University of Michigan, Liu Lab (Jie Liu) 01/2026 – 05/2026
  • Literature-writing agent — multi-turn, rubric-grounded, multi-dimensional rewards spread across a full trajectory, for an RL post-training task with no binary reward signal.
  • Biomedical deep-research agent — training-free, multi-module agent (compress-before-reason + verbatim-grounded answers behind a mechanical faithfulness gate); fact-level completeness 0.442→0.645, entity coverage 0.676→0.823, 55% lower cost, zero fabricated citations. (deploying at glkb.org)
  • KV-cache compression & reuse — compressed reusable “Skills” into a small set of KV-cache states trained as RL-optimizable parameters; SearchQA accuracy 77.7→88.3.
  • Latent-space reasoning — designed MCIG (a training-free per-token redundancy signal) + a latent-feedback adapter; isolated four mechanistic roadblocks showing why pure latent skipping regresses at 1.5B — a documented negative result motivating selective latent↔explicit switching. code & report ↗
Efficient Reasoning & the Underthinking of RL-trained LLMs · Institute of Automation, CAS (CASIA) 06/2025 – 04/2026
  • Depth-Quality Erosion (diagnosis) — analyzed GRPO/RLVR post-training at per-reasoning-depth granularity and derived a closed-form variance-update identity, uncovering a Simpson's-paradox mode-seeking collapse: pass rate drops in every depth bin (up to −26%) even as aggregate Pass@1 rises (+2.0 pp).
  • STAGE-GRPO — a stratified-advantage variant with online per-prompt difficulty estimation that provably decouples variance dynamics from geometric alignment; eliminates the per-bin degradation and lifts MATH-500 Pass@1 to 0.868 (+4.2 pp vs. base, +2.2 pp vs. GRPO).
  • Prefix-contrastive credit assignment — concentrates the gradient on decision-critical tokens by contrasting, within a group, the branch positions where same-prefix correct and incorrect rollouts diverge (correctness verified by 19 invariant unit tests; end-to-end ablations in progress). code & report ↗
Spatiotemporal Drought Forecasting (Transformer & GraphRAG) · Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences (CAAS) 02/2025 – 06/2025
  • Designed Cross-Spatiotemporal-Former (CSTF), a cross-attention architecture fusing spatial-grid and temporal features — 98.6% drought-classification accuracy on 12 years of ERA5-Land data, outperforming LSTM baselines on long-range SPI/SPEI dependencies; built a Drought–Water–Wheat GraphRAG system (DistilBERT + FAISS + Baichuan2-13B) for evidence-based answers.
